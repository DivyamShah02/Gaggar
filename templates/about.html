MEET THE MANAGING DIRECTOR

Shubhankar Gattani

As an accomplished Director with a proven track record in the management consulting industry, He brings a wealth of expertise in Operations Management, Sales, Marketing, and Product Development to the plastic packaging and PET industry. With several years of experience under his belt, He currently holds the role of Director and Head of Product Management and Operations at both Amicale Bizcom and GEPL. In these capacities, He lead a dynamic, cross-functional team dedicated to innovating next-generation PET preforms that are revolutionizing the production of high-quality PET-related products.


At the core of his professional ethos is a passion for crafting superior, trusted products that empower individuals to achieve their career aspirations. His commitment to excellence extends beyond the technical aspects of product development; it encompasses a broader mission to ensure that our offerings positively impact the lives and careers of those who use them. By leveraging his comprehensive skill set and industry experience, He strives to drive the continued success of the company, maintaining a steadfast focus on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

We aim to become an indispensable partner to our clients by delivering distinguished goods and services that align with their objectives. By offering a wide array of well-crafted and affordable flexible packaging solutions, we strive to enable as many businesses as possible to benefit from our offerings.

Our commitment is to craft the finest products without causing any unnecessary harm. We are not limited by conventional practices; our business is dedicated to preserving the environment.
