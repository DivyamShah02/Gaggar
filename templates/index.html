Why Us?

We are one of the leading manufacturers of PET-preforms serving multiple industries for the last 1 and a half decades. We provide a wide array of PET-preforms to various beverages, edible oils, and various other groups across the nation. With strong experience in plastic packaging, PetNext has expanded its product line and range of services immensely over the years. We have the potential to manufacture 800 Tons of PET-preforms across various industries and applications, and PetNext believes in expanding this further over the years.
